Welcome to PVPUltimate!

We are aiming to create an epic PVP server, with 10+ different maps that you can choose from!

With a wide selection of kits, as well as buyable gear from the shop, you can quickly progress by getting kills.

This server contains special maps, that include the space map and the in-progress volcano and thunderstorm maps.

Become a wizard or witch by brewing potions. The ingredients can be found at the brewing merchant.

Elytra Battles are now out! Duel with swords and bows mid-flight as you try to not fall into the lava!

Play our minigames, such as TNT Spleef or Knockback Blitz!

Farm materials by mining and fighting enemies! If there isn't a spot for you in the mining camps, you better fight for one!
